# Privacy Policy for ApexBox Companion

**Last Updated:** October 21, 2025

## Introduction

ApexBox Companion ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, display name, and profile picture
- **Authentication Data**: Login credentials managed securely through Firebase Authentication
- **User-Generated Content**: Vehicle information (make, model, year, nickname)

### Performance Data
- **Telemetry Data**: Speed, G-force, temperature, altitude collected from your OBD-II device
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates during performance sessions for track replay features
- **Session Data**: Driving session logs, statistics, and CSV exports

### Device Information
- **Bluetooth Data**: Connection status and device identifiers for OBD-II pairing
- **Device Identifiers**: Unique device ID for analytics and crash reporting
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, feature usage, and preferences

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Service Delivery**
   - Provide real-time telemetry display and analysis
   - Store and retrieve driving session history
   - Enable crew leaderboards and social features

2. **App Improvement**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve features
   - Debug crashes and fix technical issues
   - Optimize performance and user experience

3. **Communication**
   - Send important service updates
   - Notify about new features
   - Respond to support requests

## Data Storage and Security

- **Firebase Services**: We use Google Firebase for:
  - Authentication (Firebase Auth)
  - Database storage (Cloud Firestore)
  - File storage (Firebase Storage)

- **Security Measures**:
  - End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  - Secure authentication with industry standards
  - Regular security audits and updates

- **Data Retention**: We retain your data while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Firebase/Google Cloud**: For hosting and infrastructure
- **Analytics Providers**: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights

### Crew Features
When you join a crew:
- Your display name and performance stats become visible to crew members
- Crew leaderboards display your ranking and top speeds
- You can leave a crew at any time to stop sharing this data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Correction**: Update incorrect or incomplete information
3. **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
4. **Portability**: Export your data in a machine-readable format
5. **Opt-Out**: Disable location tracking or delete the app

### How to Exercise Your Rights
- In-app: Settings → Profile → Delete Account
- Email: privacy@apexbox.com
- Response time: Within 30 days

## Location Data

- **Permission**: We request location access only when in use
- **Usage**: GPS data is collected only during active performance sessions
- **Control**: You can disable location services in device settings
- **Opt-Out**: Sessions will still record OBD-II data without GPS

## Bluetooth Data

- **Purpose**: Connect to OBD-II performance tracking devices
- **Storage**: Device pairing information stored locally
- **Security**: No sensitive vehicle data transmitted to servers

## Children's Privacy

ApexBox Companion is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses:

- **Firebase** (Google): Authentication, database, storage
- **Expo**: App development and deployment platform
- **React Native**: Mobile app framework

These services have their own privacy policies:
- Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in:
- United States (Firebase servers)
- Your local device (cached data)

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## Cookies and Tracking

We use:

- **Analytics**: Aggregated usage statistics (anonymized)
- **No Advertising**: We do not use tracking cookies for ads
